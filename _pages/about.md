Welcome to my website. I am a PhD student in the Texas A&M Econ program. [cite_start]My current research focuses on empirical macroeconomics, exchange rate determination, and financial economics [cite: 51][cite_start], with a particular emphasis on Bayesian time series models, stochastic volatility [cite: 62][cite_start], and uncertainty-driven density forecasting[cite: 56, 63]. Working Papers
======
* [cite_start]**Endogenous Stochastic Volatility Selection in Bayesian VARs** (with Kyu Ho Kang) [cite: 14]
  * [cite_start]*Summary*: Develops a Bayesian VAR model with stochastic volatility and spike-and-slab selection (BVAR-SVVS) that endogenously determines whether each equation should feature time-varying or constant volatility, yielding substantial improvements in density forecast performance[cite: 14].

* [cite_start]**Climate Risk and the Macroeconomy: Evidence from a LBVAR with Stochastic Volatility** [cite: 15]
  * [cite_start]*Summary*: Examines how incorporating climate variables improves macro-financial forecasting fit, using a large Bayesian VAR (LBVAR) framework[cite: 16]. [cite_start]Results show that including climate risk notably enhances long-term predictive accuracy, particularly for housing prices and financial indicators in Korea[cite: 17].
